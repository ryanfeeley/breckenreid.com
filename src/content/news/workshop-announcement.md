## Learn the Wheel

We are excited to announce a beginner's intensive weekend workshop. Whether you are a complete novice or looking to refine your centering technique, this workshop will provide hands-on guidance.

**Topics Covered:**
1.  Wedging and clay preparation
2.  Centering on the wheel
3.  Pulling walls and shaping
4.  Trimming and finishing

Spaces are limited to 8 participants. Link in bio to register!
